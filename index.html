That's Me!

Hi there! I'm Shivesh Kumar from Massachusetts, US. I am currently pursuing my masters in Information Systems at Northeastern University, Boston with running GPA of 4.0. I have earned my Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science and Engineering from Visvesvaraya Technological University, Bengaluru, India. Presently working as a Graduate Teaching Assistant for Web Design and User Experience course.

I am an agile software developer. Have worked with Accenture for 3 years as a full stack developer. I have a demonstrated history of working in CMT (Communication and Media Technology) and E-Commerce domains. Developed end to end applications using multiple frameworks for both front-end and back-end, also have knowledge of full software development life cycle from Solution Design walkthrough until production support. While I was working I developed a couple of automation tools for my client which has eliminated manual effort and risk of human errors. I am a dynamic, collaborative, and very much curious to build new experiences and intend to become a more passionate full stack developer now.
